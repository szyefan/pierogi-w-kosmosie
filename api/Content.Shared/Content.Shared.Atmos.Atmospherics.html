Tmax

Do not allow any gas mixture temperatures to exceed this number. It is occasionally possible to have very small heat capacity (e.g. room that was just unspaced) and for large amounts of energy to be transferred to it, even for a brief moment. However, this messes up subsequent calculations and so cap it here. The physical interpretation is that at this temperature, any gas that you would have transforms into plasma.
